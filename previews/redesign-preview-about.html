Biography

Ergys Zhabjaku is an Albanian photographer working across fine art, editorial, and commercial disciplines. Born in Tirana in 1991, he studied visual arts before developing a practice rooted in the tension between landscape and identity — exploring how place shapes image and image reshapes memory.

His work is characterised by long exposures, restrained palettes, and an editorial precision that moves between the intimate and the monumental. Campaigns and commissions coexist with ongoing personal series.

Editorial clients include Icon Magazine, Living Magazine, and various Albanian cultural institutions. Commercial work spans fashion, architecture, and brand identity.
