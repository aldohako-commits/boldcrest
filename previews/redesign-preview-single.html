Prani

Art — Solo Exhibition 2025

Prani is a solo exhibition presented in Tirana, exploring the intersection of portraiture and natural light. The series captures subjects in moments of unguarded stillness, using long exposures and restrained palettes to reveal the quiet tension between presence and absence.

Shot across three months in the Albanian countryside, the project examines how landscape shapes identity and how the photographic frame transforms lived experience into memory. Each image is a meditation on time — the slow accumulation of light on film, the gradual erosion of certainty.
