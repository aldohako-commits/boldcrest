# Albanian Copywriting Style Guides — Index

> **How to use:** Choose the style guide that matches your copy type. Each guide has its own voice profile, headline formulas, vocabulary, patterns, and checklist. Combine guides when needed (e.g., a sports brand launch = sports-energy + commercial-promotional).

---

## Available Style Guides

### 1. News & Editorial (`style-news-editorial.md`)
- **Use for:** Press releases, corporate announcements, thought leadership, LinkedIn, newsletters, product launch announcements
- **Tone:** Authoritative, balanced, professional
- **Model outlet:** Top Channel
- **Key features:** ka+participle tense, attribution verb variety, hedging patterns, 4 headline formulas, 3 opening patterns

### 2. Bold & Provocative (`style-bold-provocative.md`)
- **Use for:** Disruptive brands, social media ads, challenger brands, youth campaigns, guerrilla marketing, viral content
- **Tone:** Confrontational, passionate, punchy
- **Model outlet:** Balkanweb
- **Key features:** Proverb-infused headlines, exclamatory fragments, negation as power (S', Jo, Mos), folk wisdom hooks, short sharp CTAs

### 3. Commercial & Promotional (`style-commercial-promotional.md`)
- **Use for:** Product descriptions, e-commerce, ad copy, landing pages, promotional emails, job postings, B2B proposals
- **Tone:** Warm, confident, benefit-focused
- **Model outlet:** Shqiptarja lifestyle + business events
- **Key features:** Native promotional vocabulary, B2B vs B2C register differences, trust signals, product description structure, seasonal triggers

### 4. Cultural & Heritage (`style-cultural-heritage.md`)
- **Use for:** Tourism, heritage brands, luxury, museums, cultural institutions, artisan products, diaspora campaigns
- **Tone:** Evocative, proud, poetic
- **Model source:** Mann Dictionary emotional vocabulary + cultural reporting
- **Key features:** Untranslatable emotion words (mall, besë, nder, vatër), sensory language, rhythmic patterns, hospitality formulas, compound word systems

### 5. Sports & Energy (`style-sports-energy.md`)
- **Use for:** Sports brands, fitness, gaming, competitions, youth lifestyle, motivational content
- **Tone:** Passionate, urgent, tribal
- **Model outlet:** Sports pages of all three outlets
- **Key features:** Action verb arsenal, duke+participle cinematic progressive, us-vs-them framing, subjunctive rallying, numbers for impact

### 6. Lifestyle & Social Media (`style-lifestyle-social.md`)
- **Use for:** Social media posts, Instagram captions, influencer partnerships, DTC brands, beauty/fashion copy, relationship/wellness content, email newsletters, quiz/listicle content
- **Tone:** Intimate, warm, confessional, conspiratorial
- **Model outlets:** Anabel Magazine, Revista Who, Kryefjala
- **Key features:** Confessional headlines, "Në një kohë kur" zeitgeist opener, lifestyle attribution verbs (rrëfen, flet hapur), emotional vocabulary arsenal, fashion/beauty loanwords, sponsored content patterns, diaspora pride hooks, social media vocabulary

## Combining Styles

Most real-world copy needs elements from multiple guides:

| Copy Type | Primary Guide | Secondary Guide |
|-----------|--------------|-----------------|
| Product launch press release | News & Editorial | Commercial & Promotional |
| Social media ad (consumer) | Lifestyle & Social Media | Commercial & Promotional |
| Instagram/influencer campaign | Lifestyle & Social Media | Bold & Provocative |
| Tourism landing page | Cultural & Heritage | Commercial & Promotional |
| Sports brand campaign | Sports & Energy | Bold & Provocative |
| Corporate social responsibility | News & Editorial | Cultural & Heritage |
| Diaspora emotional campaign | Cultural & Heritage | Lifestyle & Social Media |
| Job posting | Commercial & Promotional | News & Editorial |
| Event promotion | Commercial & Promotional | Sports & Energy |
| Beauty/fashion brand | Lifestyle & Social Media | Commercial & Promotional |
| Celebrity endorsement | Lifestyle & Social Media | Bold & Provocative |
| Email newsletter (consumer) | Lifestyle & Social Media | Commercial & Promotional |
| B2B thought leadership | News & Editorial | Commercial & Promotional |
